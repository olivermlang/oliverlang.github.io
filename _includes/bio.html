Oliver Lang

Doctoral candidate, University of Wisconsin-Madison

My research sits at the intersection of comparative politics, political communication, and political economy.

I study the conditions under which media markets under authoritarian rule provide citizens with critical and accurate coverage of political events and incumbent leaders. In my dissertation research, I explain how regime institutions shape the production of televesion news across fifty-seven autocratic regimes.
